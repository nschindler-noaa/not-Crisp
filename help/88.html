FLUSH Transport Merit Equation: FLUSH Model

Selecting BehaviorFLUSH Transport Merit Equation opens a window for setting the parameters used in the Simple Mortality Model based on the FLUSH Model (see COMPASS in Context with Other Models). To use these parameter values during a run, select Simple Mortality for the Mortality Model in RunRuntime Settings. The Y-axis is the merit of transport value D and the X-axis is the percent survival. There are four choices for the FLUSH Transport Merit Equation--used in PATH for spring chinook (Marmorek 1996)--where:

  • a: Constant known per species.
  • b: Constant known per species.
  • Vt: Direct survival of transported fish.
