Velocity: River velocity in a reach

Selecting a Reach from ReservoirVelocity opens a histogram of the daily river velocity in miles per day for the reach for viewing only. The river velocity through a reach is calculated based on water elevation, bathymetry, and the daily flow level in the segment. Bathymetry data is read in to COMPASS from the River Description File (columbia.desc).

This is a Julian Day Output window.
